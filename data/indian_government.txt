Structure:
- India is a federal parliamentary democratic republic.
- President is Head of State
- Prime Minister is Head of Government

Union Government:
- Three branches: Legislature, Executive, Judiciary

Parliament:
- Lok Sabha: Lower House
- Rajya Sabha: Upper House

PMO:
- Assists Prime Minister in policymaking and administration

Judiciary:
- Supreme Court (highest court)
- High Courts
- District Courts

Election Commission:
- Conducts elections in India
- Ensures fairness & transparency

Important Positions:
- President of India
- Vice President
- Prime Minister
- Chief Justice of India
- Cabinet Ministers

Constitutional Bodies:
- UPSC
- CAG
- Finance Commission
- EC (Election Commission)

Policies:
- Digital India
- Make in India
- Skill India
- Beti Bachao Beti Padhao
