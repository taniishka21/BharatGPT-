Indian Economy:
- 5th largest economy in the world (as of 2024)
- Sectors: Agriculture, Industry, Services
- Services sector contributes most to GDP

Major Government Schemes:
- Make in India
- Digital India
- Startup India
- PM Jan Dhan Yojana
- PM Awas Yojana

Agriculture:
- Major crops: Wheat, Rice, Sugarcane, Cotton
- India is the world’s largest producer of milk and pulses

Industry:
- Automobile, Steel, Textiles, Pharmaceuticals
- India is one of the largest IT exporters

Services:
- IT industry contributes heavily to GDP
- Bangalore known as the “Silicon Valley of India”

Startups:
- India has 100+ unicorns
- Major unicorns: Byju’s, OYO, Zomato, Swiggy, Paytm
