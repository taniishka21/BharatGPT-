INDIA HISTORY – COMPLETE OVERVIEW

1. ANCIENT INDIA (3300 BCE – 1200 CE)
- Indus Valley Civilization: One of the world’s earliest urban civilizations (Harappa & Mohenjo-Daro).
- Features: Town planning, drainage system, granaries, Great Bath, seals, weights, pottery.
- Vedic Age: Aryans composed the Vedas – Rigveda, Yajurveda, Samaveda, Atharvaveda.
- Society based on varna system, agriculture, iron tools.
- Important kingdoms: Magadha, Kosala, Kalinga, Maurya Empire.
- Jainism by Mahavira (24th Tirthankara), Buddhism by Gautam Buddha.

2. MAURYA EMPIRE (322–185 BCE)
- Founded by Chandragupta Maurya with help of Chanakya.
- Capital: Pataliputra.
- Ashoka the Great: Kalinga War, promoted Buddhism, rock & pillar edicts.

3. GUPTA EMPIRE (320–550 CE)
- Golden Age of India: advances in science, mathematics, art, literature.
- Aryabhatta: invented zero, explained earth’s rotation.
- Kalidasa: famous Sanskrit poet.
- Trade flourished, Hindu culture expanded.

4. MEDIEVAL INDIA (1200–1700 CE)
- Delhi Sultanate: Mamluk, Khilji, Tughlaq, Sayyid, Lodi dynasties.
- Important rulers: Qutub-ud-din Aibak, Alauddin Khilji, Muhammad bin Tughlaq.
- Mughal Empire: Founded by Babur in 1526 (First Battle of Panipat).
- Akbar: religious tolerance, mansabdari system, Fatehpur Sikri.
- Shah Jahan: Taj Mahal, Red Fort.
- Aurangzeb: largest expansion of Mughal empire.

5. MARATHA EMPIRE
- Founded by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
- Known for guerrilla warfare, strong navy, forts like Raigad, Pratapgad.

6. COLONIAL INDIA (1757–1947)
- Battle of Plassey (1757): British East India Company rule begins.
- Social reforms: Raja Ram Mohan Roy (ended Sati), Ishwar Chandra Vidyasagar (widow remarriage).
- Revolt of 1857: first war of independence.

7. INDIAN FREEDOM STRUGGLE
- INC formed in 1885.
- Mahatma Gandhi: Non-Cooperation, Dandi March, Quit India Movement.
- Other freedom fighters: Bhagat Singh, Sardar Patel, Bose, Rani Lakshmibai, Lala Lajpat Rai.
- Subhash Chandra Bose formed INA.
- Jallianwala Bagh massacre (1919).
- Partition of Bengal, Swadeshi movement.
- Independence on 15 August 1947.

8. MODERN INDIA
- First PM: Jawaharlal Nehru.
- Green & White Revolution.
- Economic liberalization 1991.
- India develops into modern economy with technology and space achievements.
