Indian Railways:
- 4th largest railway network in the world
- Operated by Ministry of Railways
- 14 lakh+ employees (8th largest employer globally)

Zones:
- 19 Railway Zones across India
- Northern, Central, Western, Southern zones etc.

Major Trains:
- Rajdhani Express
- Shatabdi Express
- Vande Bharat Express
- Duronto Express
- Gatimaan Express

Heritage Railways:
- Darjeeling Himalayan Railway
- Nilgiri Mountain Railway
- Kalka–Shimla Railway

Achievements:
- Vande Bharat: India’s semi-high-speed train
- Dedicated Freight Corridor (DFC)

Ticketing:
- IRCTC handles online ticket booking for railways
