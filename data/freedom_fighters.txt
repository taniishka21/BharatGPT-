Mahatma Gandhi:
- Leader of Indian independence movement
- Introduced non-violence (Ahimsa) and Satyagraha
- Led Dandi March, Quit India Movement

Subhash Chandra Bose:
- Founder of Indian National Army (INA)
- Famous slogan: “Give me blood, and I will give you freedom”

Bhagat Singh:
- Revolutionary freedom fighter
- Involved in Lahore Conspiracy Case
- Became a symbol of courage for Indian youth

Rani Lakshmibai:
- Queen of Jhansi
- Led rebellion in 1857 Revolt
- Fought bravely against British forces

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel:
- Iron Man of India
- Unified 562 princely states into India

Jawaharlal Nehru:
- India’s first Prime Minister
- Prominent leader in independence movement

Lal Bahadur Shastri:
- Second PM of India
- Gave slogan “Jai Jawan Jai Kisan”

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar:
- Architect of Indian Constitution
- Fought for social equality and rights

Mangal Pandey:
- Key figure in the 1857 Revolt
- His rebellion inspired the first war of independence
