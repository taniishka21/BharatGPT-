Indian Education System:
- Managed by Ministry of Education

Structure:
- Primary (1-5)
- Upper Primary (6-8)
- Secondary (9-10)
- Higher Secondary (11-12)
- Higher Education (College, University)

Types of Boards:
- CBSE
- ICSE
- State Boards

Major Exams:
- JEE Main & Advanced (Engineering)
- NEET (Medical)
- UPSC CSE
- GATE
- CAT

IITs:
- Top engineering institutes
- 23 IITs across India

NITs:
- 31 NITs offering engineering programs

Scholarships:
- PM Scholarship Scheme
- National Means cum Merit

New Education Policy (NEP 2020):
- 5+3+3+4 education structure
- Focus on skill development & coding
