Hinduism:
- Oldest religion
- Multiple deities: Shiva, Vishnu, Durga, Ganesha

Islam:
- Second largest religion in India
- Followers called Muslims

Christianity:
- Introduced by St. Thomas the Apostle in 52 AD

Sikhism:
- Founded by Guru Nanak
- Holy book: Guru Granth Sahib

Buddhism:
- Founded by Gautama Buddha
- Four Noble Truths, Eightfold Path

Jainism:
- Founded by Lord Mahavira
- Principles: Ahimsa, Satya, Asteya

Zoroastrianism:
- Followers known as Parsis
- Worship fire

Judaism:
- Smallest population in India
