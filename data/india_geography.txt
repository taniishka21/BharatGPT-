INDIA GEOGRAPHY – COMPLETE OVERVIEW

1. LOCATION & AREA
- India is the 7th largest country by area.
- Lies in South Asia.
- Bordered by Pakistan, Nepal, China, Bhutan, Bangladesh, Myanmar.

2. PHYSICAL DIVISIONS OF INDIA
- Himalayan Mountains
- Northern Plains (Indo-Gangetic plain)
- Peninsular Plateau
- Coastal Plains
- Thar Desert
- Islands (Andaman-Nicobar & Lakshadweep)

3. MAJOR RIVERS
- Ganga (longest)
- Yamuna
- Brahmaputra
- Godavari
- Krishna
- Narmada
- Kaveri
- Indus (partly)

4. IMPORTANT MOUNTAINS
- Himalayas: world’s youngest fold mountains.
- Mount Everest (Nepal border)
- Kanchenjunga (India's highest peak)
- Aravalli: oldest mountain range.

5. CLIMATE
- Tropical monsoon climate.
- Seasons: Summer, Monsoon, Winter.
- Monsoon winds bring rainfall.

6. NATURAL RESOURCES
- Coal, iron ore, bauxite, mica, limestone.
- Major agricultural products: rice, wheat, sugarcane, cotton.

7. STATES & UNION TERRITORIES
- 28 States & 8 Union Territories.
- Largest State: Rajasthan
- Smallest State: Goa
- Most populous: Uttar Pradesh

8. BIODIVERSITY HOTSPOTS
- Western Ghats
- Eastern Himalayas
- Sundarbans mangroves
