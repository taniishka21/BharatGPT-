India’s Scientific Progress:
- India ranks among top nations in scientific research output.
- Major fields: Space, Biotechnology, IT, Pharmaceuticals, Nuclear Science.

Notable Scientists:
- C.V. Raman: Raman Effect, Nobel Prize in Physics (1930)
- Homi J. Bhabha: Father of Indian nuclear program
- Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam: Missile development, ISRO & DRDO
- S. Chandrasekhar: Nobel Prize in Physics (1983)
- Venkatraman Ramakrishnan: Nobel Prize in Chemistry (2009)

Technological Achievements:
- Aadhaar: World’s largest biometric system
- UPI: Global standard in digital payments
- CoWIN: Used for COVID vaccine management
- 5G rollout and indigenous 6G research

Biotechnology:
- India is major producer of generic medicines
- Covaxin: India’s indigenous COVID-19 vaccine

Nuclear Science:
- India operates 22 nuclear reactors
- Major centers: BARC, Kalpakkam, Tarapur

IT Sector:
- Exports: $200+ billion annually
- Major hubs: Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai
