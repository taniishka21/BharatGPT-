INDIAN CULTURE – COMPLETE OVERVIEW

1. INDIAN VALUES
- Unity in diversity, family bonding, respect, hospitality (“Atithi Devo Bhava”).
- Ayurveda, yoga, classical music, ancient knowledge systems.

2. MAJOR RELIGIONS
- Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, Sikhism originated in India.
- Islam, Christianity, Zoroastrianism also have deep roots.

3. TRADITIONAL CLOTHING
- Women: Saree, salwar kameez, lehenga.
- Men: Kurta, sherwani, dhoti, lungi.
- Regional variations like Kerala Kasavu, Banarasi silk, Kanjeevaram.

4. INDIAN FESTIVALS
- Diwali: festival of lights.
- Holi: festival of colors.
- Navratri & Durga Puja.
- Eid-ul-Fitr, Eid-ul-Adha.
- Christmas, Onam, Pongal, Baisakhi.
- Ganesh Chaturthi, Raksha Bandhan, Makar Sankranti.

5. INDIAN FOOD
- Masalas: turmeric, cumin, coriander, cardamom.
- Popular dishes: Biryani, dosa, idli, roti, paneer, thali, samosa.
- Regional cuisines: Punjabi, Bengali, Gujarati, South Indian, Rajasthani, Goan.

6. CLASSICAL DANCES
- Bharatanatyam (Tamil Nadu)
- Kathak (North India)
- Odissi (Odisha)
- Kathakali & Mohiniyattam (Kerala)
- Manipuri (Manipur)
- Kuchipudi (Andhra Pradesh)

7. MUSIC
- Hindustani classical (North)
- Carnatic classical (South)
- Instruments: sitar, tabla, veena, mridangam.

8. ARCHITECTURE
- Temples: Khajuraho, Konark, Rajasthan havelis.
- Mughal: Taj Mahal, Humayun’s tomb.
- Buddhist: Sanchi Stupa, Ajanta & Ellora caves.

9. LANGUAGES
- 22 official languages including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi.
- Script diversity: Devanagari, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Gujarati, Gurmukhi.

10. INDIAN FAMILY SYSTEM
- Joint family traditions, arranged marriage system, cultural rituals.
