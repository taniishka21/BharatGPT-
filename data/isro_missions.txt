Chandrayaan-1 (2008):
- India’s first lunar mission
- Discovered water molecules on the Moon

Chandrayaan-2 (2019):
- Orbiter + Vikram Lander + Pragyan Rover
- Orbiter still functioning

Chandrayaan-3 (2023):
- India became the first country to land on Moon’s South Pole
- Lander: Vikram, Rover: Pragyan

Mangalyaan / Mars Orbiter Mission (2013):
- India became the first Asian country to reach Mars orbit
- Achieved in the first attempt

Aditya-L1 (2023):
- India’s first mission to study the Sun
- Positioned at Lagrange point L1

Gaganyaan (Upcoming):
- India’s first human space flight mission
- Astronauts (Vyomnauts) selected & training underway

PSLV (Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle):
- ISRO’s most reliable rocket
- Famous for cost-effective multiple satellite launches

GSLV Mk-III (LVM-3):
- Heavy lift launch vehicle
- Used for Chandrayaan-2 and 3

Astrosat:
- India’s first dedicated astronomy space observatory
